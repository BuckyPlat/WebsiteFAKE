About Us

Driven by innovation, passion, and a legacy of performance

By NissanGlazer Editorial Team

Updated: 7 October 2025

Our History: The Spirit of Nissan Motorsport

Since its founding in 1933, Nissan has always stood for innovation and performance. In the 1960s, the brand began its journey into motorsport under the “Nissan Motorsport International” division, better known today as NISMO. This department was created to bring racing technology and engineering excellence directly to the road.

From rally racing to circuit dominance, Nissan’s motorsport history is filled with legendary victories and technological breakthroughs. The fusion of engineering and passion led to the birth of one of the world’s most celebrated performance cars — the GT-R.

The Birth of the R32 GT-R: A New Era Begins

In 1989, Nissan unveiled the Skyline GT-R R32, a car that changed the face of performance motoring forever. Powered by the iconic RB26DETT engine and the revolutionary ATTESA E-TS all-wheel-drive system, the R32 brought race-grade technology to everyday roads.

The R32 dominated Japan’s Group A racing and earned the nickname “Godzilla” for its unmatched power and precision on the track. Its engineering DNA continues to influence Nissan’s modern GT-R models — a true testament to its timeless legacy.

Our Home: Nissan Yokohama Factory

The beating heart of Nissan’s engineering excellence lies at the Yokohama Plant — where engines like the RB26DETT were born and perfected. Located in Kanagawa, Japan, this factory remains a symbol of craftsmanship and innovation.
